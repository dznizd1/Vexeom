VEXEOM — PLATFORM BRIEF

Vexeom is infrastructure.
Not a product.

Every layer of aviation has been systematically digitised and made intelligent — except one. The human performance layer. Vexeom is the operating system for that layer. It does not advise. It does not counsel. It identifies, maps, and delivers scored intelligence on the risk factors that determine whether a pilot career survives contact with the industry.

THE CATEGORY

The gap nobody filled.

Palantir built the data infrastructure for governments and defence organisations. Anduril built the autonomous systems layer for the battlefield. Each identified a layer of human activity that had never been made systematically intelligent — and built the infrastructure to fill that gap.

Aviation has done the same at every layer — aircraft systems, air traffic management, flight operations, safety management, and psychometric selection. Every layer except one: the ongoing human performance sustainability of the pilots and operators on whom every outcome depends.

Psychometric screening exists — but only at selection. It fires once, at the gate, and then never again. What happens between entry and operation — the financial pressure, the fatigue accumulation, the motivation erosion, the cognitive load trajectory — is invisible. Vexeom makes it visible.

HOW IT WORKS

Pattern detection. Chain mapping. Scored intelligence.

The individual completes a structured assessment targeting 10 risk indicators. These are not wellness prompts — they are calibrated to aviation-specific failure patterns. Each response is a data point in a scoring model that weights indicators against their known compounding relationships.

Financial pressure amplifies fatigue risk. Fatigue amplifies cognitive load. Cognitive load suppresses training performance. These are documented sequences in the Vexeom failure chain library, derived from the most common non-skill-related career departure patterns in commercial aviation training.

The scored profile is mapped against this library. The system identifies which chains are active and how far they have progressed. A full intelligence report is generated instantly — containing the complete indicator profile, active chain identification, a 12-month career outlook, and prioritised recommendations ranked by urgency.

Every assessment adds to the Vexeom dataset. Over time this produces prevalence rates, intervention effectiveness data, and predictive models that no competitor can replicate. The dataset is the moat.

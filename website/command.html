WHAT COMMAND DELIVERS

No standard deliverable exists at Command level. The following represents the capability range available. Every engagement is scoped specifically through private consultation.

01
White Label Intelligence Platform
The full Vexeom intelligence framework deployed under your organisation's brand. Your workforce completes assessments through a branded interface. Reports are generated, scored, and delivered under your identity. The intelligence is Vexeom's. The presentation is yours.
02
Cohort & Population Intelligence
Aggregated, anonymised risk intelligence across your workforce. Cohort-level scoring, failure chain prevalence, systemic risk indicators, and trend analysis. Delivered as structured intelligence briefings suitable for board reporting and regulatory submissions.
03
Bespoke Indicator Development
New indicators developed specifically for your operational context — beyond the standard 10. Defence-specific performance variables, type-rating readiness indicators, or sector-specific career sustainability metrics. Built in collaboration, deployed exclusively to your engagement.
04
Data Sovereignty & Private Deployment
For organisations with data classification requirements or sovereign data obligations, Command engagements can be structured to meet those constraints. Assessment data, report outputs, and population intelligence remain within agreed jurisdictional and infrastructure boundaries.
05
Strategic Intelligence Briefings
Periodic executive-level briefings on workforce human performance trends — formatted for C-suite and board consumption. Strategic intelligence on the human layer of your organisation, mapped against industry benchmarks from the Vexeom population dataset.
ENGAGEMENT PROCESS

Command engagements follow a structured qualification and scoping process. There is no self-serve pathway. Every engagement begins with a private conversation.

01
Initial Enquiry
Submit your organisation name, role, and a brief outline of your requirements to info@vexeom.com. All enquiries are reviewed. Not all proceed to consultation.
02
Qualification Review
Vexeom reviews your enquiry against operational fit and capacity. If your organisation meets the Command criteria, you will be contacted to arrange a private consultation within 5 business days.
03
Private Consultation
A confidential scoping call to understand your organisation's requirements, workforce profile, data requirements, and strategic objectives. Pricing, deliverables, and engagement structure are discussed here — not before.
04
Engagement Proposal
A bespoke engagement proposal is issued. This document outlines scope, deliverables, timeline, commercial terms, and data handling. It is specific to your organisation.
05
Deployment
Upon agreement, the engagement is activated. Vexeom assigns a dedicated engagement contact for the duration. Reporting cadence and delivery format are agreed in the proposal stage.
